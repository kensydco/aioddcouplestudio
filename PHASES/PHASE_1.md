# PHASE 1 — AI Odd Couple Studio

**Goal:** Publish the first 4 episodes (2 weeks of content) and validate the full automated pipeline.

**Phase Duration:** 2 weeks from first episode publish date  
**Review Date:** End of Week 2 — CoS compiles Phase 1 retrospective for CEO

---

## Success Criteria

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Episodes published | 4 | 2 per week across all 3 platforms |
| Canon Guardian pass rate | 100% | All 4 episodes pass checklist |
| 3-second hook rate | ≥ 70% | Measured by YouTube Shorts retention graph at 3s |
| Average view duration | ≥ 40% | YouTube Shorts analytics |
| Budget used | ≤ $150/month | Hard cap; $120 triggers alert |
| Pipeline cycle time | ≤ 5 days | Research Monday → Publish Friday |

---

## Research Tasks

| Research Task | Assigned To | Required Output |
|---|---|---|
| Generate 10 viral AI topics | Trend_Scout | 10 topics with hook ideas |
| Analyze short-form hook patterns | Short_Form_Optimizer | Document with top hook patterns |
| Identify top beginner AI misconceptions | Episode_Architect | List of 10 misconceptions |
| Competitor channel analysis | Trend_Scout | Brief on top 5 AI education channels |

---

## Channel Plans

- **YouTube Shorts:** 2 episodes/week, optimized for search and algorithmic discovery. Primary platform for analytics.
- **TikTok:** Same 2 episodes/week, formatted with platform-specific captions (150 char max).
- **Instagram Reels:** Same 2 episodes/week, formatted with platform-specific hashtags (5–10 tags).

---

## Known Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| HeyGen avatar inconsistency | Medium | High | Manual review required for EP-001 and EP-002; build quality checklist before EP-003 |
| Budget overrun from premium models | Medium | High | CEO must approve any premium model use; $120 alert triggers immediate review |
| Hook rate below 70% | Medium | Medium | Short_Form_Optimizer reviews and rewrites hooks after EP-002 analytics available |
| Canon Guardian rejects script | Low | Medium | Episode_Architect has 1 rewrite attempt before escalating to CoS |
| HeyGen service outage | Low | High | Delay publish by 1 day; CoS notifies CEO; no workaround needed for Phase 1 |
| Platform algorithm change | Low | Low | Monitor with Trend_Scout; no action needed in Phase 1 |
