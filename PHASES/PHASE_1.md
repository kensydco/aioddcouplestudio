# PHASE 1 — AI Odd Couple Studio

**Goal:** Publish the first 4 episodes (2 weeks of content) and validate the full automated pipeline.

**Success Criteria:**
- 4 fully produced and published episodes across YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels.
- Each episode passes the Canon Guardian checklist (Milo chaotic, Gladys misnames tech, Harold mentioned, humor present, concept beginner-friendly).
- Each episode hooks the viewer in the first 3 seconds.
- Total production cost stays within the $150/month budget.

## Research Tasks
| Research Task | Assigned To | Required Output |
|---|---|---|
| Generate 10 viral AI topics | Trend_Scout | 10 topics with hook ideas |
| Analyze short-form hook patterns | Short_Form_Optimizer | Document with top hook patterns |
| Identify top beginner AI misconceptions | Episode_Architect | List of 10 misconceptions |
| Competitor channel analysis | Trend_Scout | Brief on top 5 AI education channels |

## Channel Plans
- **YouTube Shorts:** 2 episodes/week, optimized for search and algorithmic discovery.
- **TikTok:** Same 2 episodes/week, formatted with platform-specific captions.
- **Instagram Reels:** Same 2 episodes/week, formatted with platform-specific hashtags.

## Cadence
- **Monday:** Trend Scout delivers 10 topics.
- **Tuesday:** Episode Architect drafts 2 scripts.
- **Wednesday:** Canon Guardian validates, Short Form Optimizer tightens.
- **Thursday:** Visual Director creates HeyGen prompts.
- **Friday:** Publishing Manager preps metadata; auto-publish.

## Known Risks
- HeyGen avatar consistency may require manual review for the first 2 episodes.
- Budget overrun if premium models are used without escalation approval.
