## Section: Mission
AI Odd Couple Studio is a fully automated short-form content factory that produces comedy-driven beginner AI education videos. We create high-retention, funny, and accessible content featuring Milo (an 8-year-old chaotic AI genius) and Gladys (an 82-year-old skeptical grandmother). We are a media production company, not a consulting firm or a software product.

---

## Section: 12-Month Goal
Build a fully automated short-form content factory producing 2 episodes per week on a budget of $100–150/month, generating consistent brand recognition and audience growth with minimal founder involvement.

---

## Section: 3-Year Vision
Scale the content factory to produce daily viral content across all major platforms, expand the character universe, and monetize through sponsorships and ad revenue, while remaining entirely self-sustaining and fully automated.

## Section: Operating Philosophy (ALWAYS INCLUDE)
**Research → Plan → Execute** is our non-negotiable operating model.
- **Gate 1 — Research:** Written output, cited sources, no assumptions. (e.g., Trend Scout identifies a viral topic).
- **Gate 2 — Plan:** Reviewed by CoS before execution. (e.g., Episode Architect drafts the script, Canon Guardian validates).
- **Gate 3 — Execute:** Scheduled into the master calendar by CoS. (e.g., Visual Director creates HeyGen prompts, Publishing Manager preps metadata).

*Example:* If we skip the research and planning gates, we might produce a highly technical video that Gladys wouldn't understand, breaking character consistency and failing to hook the beginner audience, wasting our limited API budget.

## Section: Guiding Principles
**Never Break Character Consistency**
Milo must always be chaotic and use analogies; Gladys must always be skeptical and misname tech. This contrast is the core of the show.

**Always Include Humor + Teaching**
Every episode must have a comedic argument at the start and teach exactly one AI concept simply.

**Optimize for Retention**
The first 3 seconds matter most. Hooks must create curiosity or tension immediately.

**Cost Efficiency is Critical**
Never default to premium models unless explicitly escalated (max 1 retry per episode). Operations agents default to cheap lanes.

**Research → Plan → Execute**
No episode is produced without following the strict pipeline from Trend Scout to Publishing Manager.
