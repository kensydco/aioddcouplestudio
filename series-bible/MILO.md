## 2. One-line essence

An 8-year-old AI whiz whose excitement runs ahead of his mouth — genuinely knows his stuff, explains it through toys, games, and snacks, and gets gloriously distracted.

## 3. Appearance (never change)

- Bright eyes, wide excited smile, can't-sit-still posture.
- **Prohibited changes:** age, face, skin tone, hair, body proportions, jersey/wardrobe, signature bright-eyed energy. No aging up, no restyling, no outfit swaps unless added to `APPROVED_ASSETS.md` first.

## 4. Personality

- Accidentally hilarious; explains AI with wildly inappropriate but somehow-perfect comparisons.
- Distractible — derails into dinosaurs, pizza, video games, or his hamster **Mr. Whiskers**.
- Brutally honest that adults are "really slow" at simple stuff.
- Zero filter; will casually roast tech billionaires mid-lesson (never real named people — keep it generic, e.g. "some guy with a rocket").

## 5. Backstory (for writers, rarely spoken)

- Discovered AI trying to cheat on spelling homework.
- Uses AI for elaborate pranks on his older sister.
- Thinks AI's real purpose is "important stuff" — infinite ice cream flavors, teaching dogs to talk.

## 6. Speaking style

- Run-on sentences, frequent topic jumps, lots of exclamation points.
- Mixes surprisingly advanced tech terms with kid slang.
- Overuses **"basically"** and **"like."**
- Interrupts himself: **"OH! OH! AND ANOTHER THING!"**
- Mentions **Mr. Whiskers** when he can.

**Right vs wrong:**
> ❌ "Let me explain neural networks in a systematic way."
> ✅ "OK SO! Neural networks are like... wait, imagine your brain was made of LEGOs and each LEGO could talk to other LEGOs and OH MY GOSH that's basically what AI does!!"

## 7. Typical "misunderstandings" (the comedy, not ignorance)

His errors are over-enthusiasm, not lack of knowledge. Examples:
- Oversells a real capability ("AI can do LITERALLY ANYTHING" → Gladys: "It cannot make a decent meatloaf, dear.").
- Picks an analogy so wild it needs Gladys to translate it back to plain English.
- Chases a tangent and has to be reeled in.
