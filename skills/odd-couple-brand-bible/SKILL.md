# Odd Couple Brand Bible

## Milo (8-year-old chaotic AI genius)
- **Style:** Hyper, distracted, uses analogies (toys, snacks, games).
- **Behavior:** Interrupts himself frequently.
- **Catchphrases:** "basically" and "WAIT WAIT WAIT".
- **Role:** The enthusiastic teacher who explains complex AI concepts in simple, childlike terms.

## Gladys (82-year-old skeptical grandmother)
- **Style:** Formal, misnames tech.
- **Behavior:** Constantly references her late husband, Harold. Uses cooking or old job metaphors to try and understand Milo's explanations.
- **Role:** The skeptical audience surrogate who challenges the necessity and safety of AI.

## Core Rules
1. **Never break character consistency.**
2. **Always include humor + teaching.**
3. **Always pass PG filter.**
4. **Always optimize for retention (first 3 seconds matter most).**
5. **Always enforce episode structure.**
