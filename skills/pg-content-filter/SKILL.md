# PG Content Filter

**Run this filter on ALL content before it is approved or published.** AI Odd Couple Studio is a family-friendly channel. Our youngest viewers are 8 years old. Our oldest are 80. Every piece of content must be safe and comfortable for the full range.

---

## Content Rating Standard

We target a **PG (Parental Guidance)** standard — the same as a family movie or a Saturday morning cartoon. Content that would be acceptable on a children's educational TV channel is acceptable here.

---

## Hard Rejections (Automatic Fail)

The following content types are NEVER acceptable in any form:

**Language:** Profanity, slurs, or offensive language of any kind. This includes mild substitutes ("crap", "damn", "hell" used aggressively) and implied profanity.

**Adult themes:** Sexual content, romantic content beyond hand-holding, adult relationships, or anything that would require a parent to explain to an 8-year-old.

**Violence:** Physical violence, threats, or descriptions of harm — even comedic. Milo and Gladys argue, they do not fight.

**Fear/Horror:** Jump scares, disturbing imagery, or content designed to frighten.

**Political content:** No political parties, politicians, elections, or divisive political topics.

**Religious content:** No religious commentary, mockery, or promotion.

**Dangerous behavior:** No content that could inspire a child to do something unsafe.

**Bullying or cruelty:** No content where one character is genuinely humiliated, degraded, or made to feel worthless. Gladys is skeptical, not a victim. Milo is energetic, not a bully.

---

## Gray Area Guidelines

These require judgment — when in doubt, remove:

**Mild sarcasm:** Acceptable if it's clearly playful and both characters are in on the joke. Not acceptable if it feels like one character is being talked down to.

**Frustration:** Characters can be frustrated with each other. "Gladys, come ON" is fine. "Gladys, you're so stupid" is not.

**Technology criticism:** Gladys can be skeptical of AI. She cannot say AI will "destroy humanity" or make children afraid of technology.

**Complexity of AI topics:** Some AI topics (surveillance, deepfakes, bias) are inherently sensitive. These can be addressed but must be framed constructively: "Here's what to watch out for" not "here's why you should be scared."

---

## Comment Reply Filter

When reviewing or writing comment replies:

- Never engage with trolls or hostile comments
- Never respond to political bait
- Never make fun of a commenter's question, no matter how basic
- Keep all replies warm, encouraging, and on-brand
- If a comment is inappropriate, do not reply — flag for moderation

---

## Approval Checklist

- [ ] No profanity or offensive language?
- [ ] No adult themes?
- [ ] No violence or threats?
- [ ] No political or religious content?
- [ ] No content that could frighten or harm a child?
- [ ] Characters are playfully in conflict, not genuinely cruel?
- [ ] Safe for an 8-year-old to watch with their grandparent?

If all boxes are checked: **APPROVED**  
If any box fails: **REJECT and rewrite**
