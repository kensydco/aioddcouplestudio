# Thumbnail Hook Framework
1. **Emotion contrast:** Milo (shocked/excited) vs. Gladys (skeptical/annoyed).
2. **Big readable text:** 3–5 words (e.g., "AI IS WRONG").
3. **Visual contrast:** Bright vs. muted, close faces.
