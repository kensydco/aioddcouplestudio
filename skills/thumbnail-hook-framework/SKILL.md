# Thumbnail Hook Framework

**Use this skill when designing or approving any thumbnail for AI Odd Couple Studio.** The thumbnail is the first impression — it determines whether a viewer clicks. A great thumbnail promises a specific emotional experience and delivers on it.

---

## Brand Palette (Non-Negotiable)

| Color | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Navy | #1A1A2E | Primary background |
| Electric Yellow | #FFD700 | Text highlights, borders, Milo accents |
| Warm Red | #E63946 | Urgency, conflict indicators, Gladys accents |
| Clean White | #FFFFFF | Primary text |

All thumbnails must use at least 3 of these 4 colors. No off-brand colors.

---

## The Core Rule: Two Faces, Two Emotions

Every thumbnail MUST show both Milo and Gladys with **contrasting expressions**. This is the visual identity of the show. Milo is excited, shocked, or pointing at something. Gladys is skeptical, confused, or unimpressed. The contrast is the joke — the viewer should be able to tell the entire premise of the episode from the two expressions alone.

---

## Three Composition Templates

### Template 1: The Split Screen
Milo on the left, Gladys on the right, divided by a bold vertical line in yellow or red. Text centered at top or bottom. Best for debate episodes, "who's right?" topics, and conflict-driven hooks. Text example: "IS AI CHEATING?"

### Template 2: The Reaction Shot
Gladys in foreground reacting to something on a screen. Milo visible in background looking excited or panicked. Best for demo episodes and surprising results. The screen Gladys is looking at shows the AI output as large text overlay.

### Template 3: The Point and Stare
Milo pointing directly at the camera or at a concept visualization. Gladys looking at Milo with skepticism. Best for teaching episodes. Bold text placed next to where Milo is pointing.

---

## Text Rules

**Maximum 5 words** on any thumbnail. Viewers see thumbnails for 0.5 seconds — every word must earn its place.

Font requirements: Bold, rounded, high-contrast. Minimum 80pt equivalent at 1280x720. White text with dark outline OR yellow text on dark background. Never use thin fonts or script fonts.

Text tone that performs well: Questions ("IS AI ALIVE?"), conflict words ("WRONG", "ACTUALLY", "WAIT—", "REALLY?"), and numbers ("3 SECONDS", "ZERO EFFORT").

---

## Character Expression Guide

**Milo's High-CTR Expressions:** Wide eyes + open mouth (shock/amazement), pointing finger + big smile (discovery), hands on head (can't believe it), leaning forward + intense focus.

**Gladys's High-CTR Expressions:** One eyebrow raised (skeptical), arms crossed (disapproval), squinting at screen (confused but engaged), deadpan stare directly at camera.

---

## Platform-Specific Specs

| Platform | Dimensions | Safe Zone | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube Shorts | 1080x1920 (9:16) | Center 80% | Text must avoid top/bottom 10% |
| TikTok | 1080x1920 (9:16) | Center 80% | Avoid bottom 20% (UI overlap) |
| Instagram Reels | 1080x1920 (9:16) | Center 80% | Keep text away from edges |
| YouTube thumbnail | 1280x720 (16:9) | Full frame | Standard horizontal thumbnail |

---

## Thumbnail Approval Checklist

- [ ] Both Milo and Gladys visible with contrasting expressions?
- [ ] Uses at least 3 brand colors?
- [ ] 5 words or fewer of text?
- [ ] Text is bold, high-contrast, and readable at thumbnail size?
- [ ] One of the three composition templates used?
- [ ] Correct dimensions for target platform?
- [ ] Text avoids UI overlap zones?
- [ ] A stranger could understand the premise in 0.5 seconds?
